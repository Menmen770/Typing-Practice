A hare was making fun of the tortoise one day for being so very slow.
The tortoise smiled calmly and challenged the hare to a race right then.
The hare laughed loudly at the idea but agreed to run the course.
They chose a fox to decide the route and to signal the start of the race.
The hare sprinted so fast that he was soon far out of sight.
He sat down under a shady tree to rest and quickly fell fast asleep.
The tortoise walked on slowly and steadily never once stopping to rest.
When the hare finally woke up he saw the tortoise near the finish line.
He ran as fast as he could but he arrived too late to win.
Slow and steady wins the race in the end every single time.
