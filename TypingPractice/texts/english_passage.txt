The fox who had never seen a lion was terrified on first meeting him.
On meeting the lion a second time he merely stopped and watched from afar.
On their third meeting the fox walked straight up and greeted him warmly.
Familiarity can breed courage just as easily as it breeds contempt.
A lion lay sick in his cave unable to move for weakness.
The animals came one by one to pay their respects to the great king.
The wolf saw his chance and accused the lion of many past wrongs.
The horse heard this and gave the wolf a mighty kick that sent him flying.
The lion rose and with a single blow finished the treacherous wolf.
It is easy to kick a man when he is down but wise men never do.
