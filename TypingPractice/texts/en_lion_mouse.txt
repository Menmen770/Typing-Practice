A tiny mouse was playing near a sleeping lion in the quiet forest.
The mouse accidentally ran across the face of the lion and woke him up.
The angry lion grabbed the mouse and was about to eat him at once.
The mouse begged for mercy and promised to repay the lion someday.
The lion laughed loudly at such an idea but let the little mouse go free.
Some weeks later hunters came and trapped the lion in a strong net.
The lion roared with all his might trying to free himself but he could not.
The tiny mouse heard the distant roars and came running to help.
He chewed through the ropes of the net with his small sharp teeth.
Even the smallest and weakest friend can save you when you need help most.
