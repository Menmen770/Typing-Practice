In the summer the ant worked hard every day from morning until night.
She carried grain and stored it carefully in her underground nest.
The grasshopper sat in the cool shade singing songs all day long.
He laughed at the ant and asked why she worked so hard in the heat.
The ant said she was gathering food to prepare for the cold winter.
The grasshopper thought this was silly and went back to his singing.
When winter came the fields were covered with deep cold snow and ice.
The grasshopper had no food left and was cold and very hungry indeed.
He knocked on the door of the ant and asked for something to eat.
The ant gave him a little and told him to work harder next summer.
