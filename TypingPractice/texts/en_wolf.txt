A young shepherd boy watched over his flock of sheep on the hillside.
He grew bored and decided to play a trick on the villagers below.
He cried out wolf and all the villagers came running up the hill.
When they arrived they found no wolf and the boy laughed at them all.
A few days later he cried wolf again and the villagers came running.
They were angry to find no wolf and warned the boy not to lie.
One afternoon a real wolf appeared and began attacking the flock.
The boy cried wolf as loudly as he could but nobody came this time.
The villagers heard him but thought it was another lie and ignored him.
Nobody believes a liar even when he is finally telling the truth.
