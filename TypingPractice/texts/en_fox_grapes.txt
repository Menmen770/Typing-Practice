A hungry fox was wandering through the forest looking for something to eat.
He came upon a beautiful bunch of grapes hanging high on a vine.
The grapes looked perfectly ripe and very delicious indeed.
The fox jumped up with all his strength but could not reach the grapes.
He tried again and again but each attempt ended in failure and frustration.
After many exhausting tries he finally gave up and sat down on the ground.
He stared up at the grapes one last time with a very unhappy look.
Then he walked away and said they were probably sour anyway.
It is easy to pretend you do not want what you simply cannot have.
